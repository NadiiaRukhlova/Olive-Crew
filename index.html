Why Olive Crew

A crewing partner that stays accountable after the contract is signed.

On call at every stage

Medical exam, paperwork, contract, pre-departure briefing — we walk a seafarer from the first phone call to the gangway, so nothing falls through between agencies, clinics and shipowners.

We know our people, not just their CVs

Our database isn't a pile of résumés. We track who's on which vessel, when a contract ends, and which opening actually fits a seafarer's experience and documents.

Every match is checked before it's offered

A shipowner doesn't need "anyone with papers" — they need someone who can do the job. We verify experience, references and certificates before a candidate ever reaches your inbox.

Our core partner is Olive Shipmanagement (Athens), for whom we crew full vessels. With other shipowners, we work for years — not for a single rotation.
