About

Who we are, and why sailors and shipowners trust us

Olive Crew Management started in 2014 with two vessels and free-of-charge placement for sailors. Today that's more than 1,000 placements a year, a database of thousands of Ukrainian seafarers, and a partnership with Olive Shipmanagement (Athens) — for whom we crew full complements, mainly on bulk carriers.

Our approach

Relationships, not one-off deals

We don't work off a template. Every sailor and every shipowner is a different story, and we build relationships instead of just closing requests.

Good crewing helps build a career: we offer a higher rank at the right moment, when we see someone is ready for it, and we communicate honestly with the shipowner when something goes wrong.

License & certificates

We operate on the record

Olive Crew Management holds written confirmation from the Ministry for seafarer manning intermediary activities, and is certified to MLC 2006 and ISO 9001.
