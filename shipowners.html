For Shipowners

A reliable crew — without the hassle

We've been on the market since 2014 and place more than 1,000 sailors every year. Most of our work is on bulk carriers, alongside tankers and container ships — but the priority is always the same: quality of communication with you, quality of communication with the sailor, and quality of the result.

FAQ

Questions from shipowners

What vessel types do you work with?

Mostly bulk carriers — that's the core of our fleet experience — plus tankers and container ships, matched to your management's specific requirements.

How fast can you close an urgent vacancy?

Thanks to a database of over 20,000 sailors and continuous tracking of who's coming off contract, we can close urgent positions within a short turnaround.

Do you check candidates' experience and references?

Yes — we verify prior experience, references from previous vessels and the validity of documents before recommending anyone.

Can we order a full crew, not just individual positions?

Yes — full-complement crewing is our standard format with Olive Shipmanagement (Athens), and we offer it to other partners as well.
